About Us

  1. Home
  2. About Us

Moderna is a Product Engineering and Software Development company, equipped with a holistic approach and diverse industry expertise in Enterprise, Telecom, Artificial Intelligence and IoT sectors. With nearly a decade of knowledge and experience in project management and custom software development, we offer innovative bespoke software solutions enabling established and emerging businesses to deliver exceptional customer experience and measurable success in line with their corporate goals. Our engineering services in the communication domain make us an ideal partner for Research and Development services in this arena. We are at the forefront of building innovative solutions and services, carving a niche for ourselves, thanks to our management’s active belief in research and development. Over the years, we have worked with many B2B and B2C brands translating their needs and goals into reliable software products. Our state-of-the-art test automation facilities and infrastructure have enabled us to deliver software solutions efficiently, cost-effectively and most importantly on time with significant customer satisfaction.
